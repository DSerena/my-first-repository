How to Make

"Kimchi Fried Rice?"

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup chopped kimchi
  • 1 Cup cooked rice
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 Cups small-dice Spam or pork
  • 1/2 Cup chopped onion
  • 2 Cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 Teaspoon soy sauce
  • 3 Tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 Teaspoons vegetable oil

How to make it?

  1. Pre-heat your cast-iron skillet. Add unsalted butter, and put the garlic and onions. Stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  2. Add Kimchi and spam(or pork), until sauce is nearly dry out.
  3. Add rice into the skillet, breaking it up with spatula.
  4. Taste, and adjust the flavor with soy sauce and sesame oil. Transfer the rice into a shallow bowl.
  5. Place a separate nonstick pan over medium heat, and add vegetable oil. When the pan became hot, add eggs with salt.
  6. Serve rice topped with fried eggs
